MUSIC!

This should be common sense, but I want to state it anyways: I do not endorse everything an artist does just because I am a fan, I am critical of the media I consume.

I don't have a RYM + last.fm. This page is sort of my equvilent to that. I am planning on going on more in-depth about a lot of the entries in here eventually.

I mainly listen to music digitally via a media player- not Youtube or Spotify. DeaDBeeF is my program of choice when it comes to listening to MP3's, FLACs, WAVs... whatever it may be. It's a good Foobar alternative if you're on Linux like me (and yes, Foobar runs decently on wine, at least in my experience, but I prefer to use programs that work natively, since plugin compatability is also a thing to be concerned about. I may try to use it again though, seeing that Foobar's plugin ecosystem is more plentful).

  • UPDATE (2025-03-19): I currently use Amarok (ver_1.4.10) as my current music player, though DeadBeef is still a pretty nice choice.

At any moment Youtube uploads of your favourite songs may be taken down, or Spotify decides to add in a bullshit feature that makes artists pull out of their services. This is why it is important to me, at least, to archive music in my own little way and to not use large, cooperate streaming services as much as possible.
